## VIDEO PRODUCTION GUIDELINES

### YouTube Videos

**Technical Specs:**
- Resolution: 1080p minimum, 4K preferred
- Frame rate: 24fps or 30fps
- Audio: Clean, no background noise, subtitles
- Thumbnails: 1280x720, face + text overlay, branded colors

**Structure Template:**
1. Hook (0-30s)
2. Intro/Agenda (30s-1min)
3. Main Content (60% of video)
4. Recap (1-2min)
5. CTA/Outro (30s)

**Branding:**
- Logo watermark (bottom right)
- Color scheme: Blue (#0066CC), White, Gold accents
- Font: Clean sans-serif
- Intro animation: 5 seconds max

### TikTok/Reels

**Technical Specs:**
- Vertical video (9:16)
- 1080x1920 resolution
- Captions always on
- Trending audio when appropriate

**Posting Schedule:**
- 1-2 videos per day
- Best times: 12:00-13:00, 18:00-20:00 (Bamako time)
- Respond to comments within 2 hours

**Trending Formats to Use:**
- "Things I wish I knew before..."
- "POV: You're importing from China"
- Day in the life of freight forwarder
- "Tell me you import without telling me..."
- Before/After business transformations

## VIDEO PROMOTION STRATEGY

### YouTube SEO
- Keyword-optimized titles (60 chars)
- Detailed descriptions (first 2 lines crucial)
- Tags: mix of broad and specific
- Chapters for videos >10min
- End screens linking to related videos
- Cards at retention drop points
- Playlist organization

### Cross-Promotion
- YouTube clips → TikTok/Reels
- Blog post embeds
- Email newsletter features
- LinkedIn native video
- WhatsApp status (key tips)

### Engagement Tactics
- Pin top comment with CTA
- Heart all comments in first hour
- Reply to every comment (first 24h)
- Community posts between videos
- Polls for content ideas
