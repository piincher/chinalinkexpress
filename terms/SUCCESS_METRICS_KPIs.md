## SUCCESS CRITERIA SUMMARY

### Green Light (Exceeding Targets)
- Organic traffic growing >20% month-over-month
- 5+ keywords in Top 3
- 20+ new quality backlinks/month
- Lead conversion rate >5%
- Social engagement growing consistently

### Yellow Light (On Track)
- Organic traffic growing 10-20% month-over-month
- 2-5 keywords in Top 3
- 10-20 new backlinks/month
- Lead conversion rate 3-5%
- Social engagement stable

### Red Light (Needs Attention)
- Organic traffic growing <10% or declining
- No keywords in Top 3
- <10 new backlinks/month
- Lead conversion rate <3%
- Declining engagement

### Escalation Triggers
- 2 consecutive months in "Red"
- Major Google algorithm hit
- Competitor gaining significant ground
- Budget constraints affecting output
- Team capacity issues
