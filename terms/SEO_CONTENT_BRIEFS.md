## WRITING GUIDELINES FOR ALL CONTENT

### Tone and Voice
- **Professional but accessible**: Expert knowledge without jargon
- **Helpful and educational**: Focus on solving problems
- **Trustworthy**: Back claims with data and examples
- **Culturally aware**: Respect for Malian business culture

### Content Quality Standards
- Every claim backed by data or experience
- Real examples from Mali market
- Updated information (check regulations quarterly)
- Clear, actionable advice
- No fluff - every sentence adds value

### French Language Guidelines
- Use formal French (vous) for business context
- Include local business terms when appropriate
- Avoid complex sentence structures
- Use active voice
- Spell out numbers 1-10, use digits for 11+

### Formatting Standards
- Short paragraphs (2-4 sentences)
- Bullet points for lists
- Bold key takeaways
- Tables for comparisons
- Visual breaks every 300 words
- Internal links every 500 words

### CTA Best Practices
- One primary CTA per 1000 words
- Contextual CTAs (relevant to content)
- Multiple contact options
- Low-friction options (PDF download, email)
- Urgency when appropriate (seasonal offers)
